## Why have a Safety Management System (SMS)

- Legal protection
- Required for licensing and insurance
- Public, Customer, Stakeholder & Staff Confidence

## What is a Safety Management System (SMS)
- EAP
- Risk Assesment
- SOP
- Logs & Registers
